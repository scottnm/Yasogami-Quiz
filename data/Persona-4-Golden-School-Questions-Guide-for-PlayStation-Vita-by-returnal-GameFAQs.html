    School Questions Guide

    Throughout the game, your teachers will quiz you or your friends on the lecture they are giving. You will also routinely taken exams. These are the answers to those questions, organized by month.

    The # column refers to which choice on the list the answer is. For example:

    =---= How many parts are there in Murakami's "The Wind-Up Bird Chronicle"?

    • Two
    • Three
    • Seven

    The correct response is Three, which is the second choice on the list of answers. Therefore, it will be listed as (2) in the # column.
